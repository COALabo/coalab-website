COALAB has been awarded a $1.2M NSF grant to study bilingual language processing in children with developmental language disorders.

This five-year project will allow us to:
1. Conduct large-scale longitudinal studies
2. Develop new assessment tools
3. Collaborate with local schools and clinics

We are excited to embark on this important work!
